Research Interests

I study the 21cm cosmology with the help of artificial intelligence (AI). Currently I apply different statistical methods to maximally extract the information from future 21 cm signal. My research interests are connecting the cosmological simulations and observations, combining multi-modal information for astrophysical problem, as well as accelerating the automatic knowledge discovery using modern statistics and machine learning in the era of big data. I plan to develop accurate and flexible cosmological and astrophysical emulators, and the corresponding inference tools; combine multi-modal information (morphology, light curves, and spectra etc.) to understand the galaxy formation and evolution; and develop flexible "cosmology-aware" symbolic algorithm to automatically find better model of our universe.
