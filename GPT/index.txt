      <h2>Welcome!</h2>
      <p>
        My research focuses on using <strong>machine learning</strong> (ML) to advance astronomy—uncovering the Milky Way’s composition and evolutionary history, probing the universe’s origin and content, and bridging these scales. As a member of the <strong>Subaru Prime Focus Spectrograph (PFS) JHU Galactic Archaeology group</strong>, I develop and apply ML methods—including foundation models—to stellar spectra for chemical abundance and radial velocity estimation, and to constrain the inner dark matter profiles of dwarf spheroidal galaxies.
      </p>
